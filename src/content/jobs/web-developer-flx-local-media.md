FLX Digital is looking for an on-site Web Developer in the Finger Lakes
region to design, optimize, and scale its web ecosystem, which supports a
media group of seven radio stations and a growing digital platform. You'll
work closely with the digital marketing director and project managers to
build, customize, and maintain WordPress websites, themes, and plugins.

The stack is centered on WordPress, PHP, HTML/CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, and
WooCommerce, with an emphasis on performance, security, SEO, and integration
APIs. Mid-to-senior level (5 years of experience preferred, Bachelor's degree
in a related field). Compensation is competitive and based on experience,
with full-time, part-time, or contract options available.
